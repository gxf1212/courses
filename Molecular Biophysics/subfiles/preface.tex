\vskip 1.5cm

\begin{tcolorbox}[title={\bfseries 课程目标}]
	\ding{228} Describe protein backbone and side-chain conformations in terms of dihedral angles and interactions\\
	\ding{228} Understand the factors that determine protein conformations\\
	\ding{228} Know common nucleic acid conformations and to understand
	the interactions that stabilize such ordered conformations\\
	\ding{228} Know the composition of cell membrane and understand unique features of membrane proteins\\
	\ding{228} Understand the structures of micelles and bilayers\\
	\ding{228} Understand how small molecules are transported across membranes\\
	\ding{228} Understand basic concepts of spectroscopic techniques: circular dichroism, fluorescence and nuclear magnetic resonance\\
	\ding{228} Be knowledgeable to the application of these techniques to life sciences\\
	\ding{228} Appreciate the use of statistical thermodynamical model to explain behavior of biological systems, like helix-coil transition in protein, structural transition in DNA\\
	\ding{228} Understand the models, learn the equilibrium and kinetic experimental approaches to study "states" in the protein folding pathway, and determine the constants\\
	\ding{228} Understand the \textit{in vivo} and \textit{in vitro} methods to study protein-ligand interaction qualitatively and quantitatively, and determine the thermaldynamical and kinetical parameters.
\end{tcolorbox}
